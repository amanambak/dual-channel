# Home Loan Policy Guidelines

## 1. Eligibility Criteria
To apply for a home loan, the customer must meet the following criteria:
- Age: 21 to 65 years.
- Employment: Must be salaried or self-employed with a stable income.
- CIBIL Score: A minimum score of 750 is preferred for best interest rates.
- Income: Minimum gross monthly salary of 25,000 INR for salaried individuals.

## 2. Loan Amount and Tenure
- Maximum Loan Amount: Up to 90% of the property value for loans up to 30 Lakhs.
- Tenure: Flexible tenure up to 30 years.

## 3. Required Documents
Customers need to provide the following documents:
- Identity Proof: PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, or Passport.
- Address Proof: Voter ID, Utility Bills, or Aadhaar Card.
- Income Proof: Last 3 months' salary slips, Form 16, and 6 months' bank statements.
- Property Documents: Sale deed, NOC from builder, and property tax receipts.

## 4. Application Process
The application process involves four main steps:
1. Application Submission: Fill out the form and submit documents.
2. Verification: The bank verifies the documents and the property.
3. Sanction: If eligible, the bank issues a sanction letter.
4. Disbursement: After legal checks, the loan amount is disbursed.
